Step 2 — Unlock flow explainer

visual
1. PIN Laptop uses 4-digit PIN as one input, not the real vault key.
2. Blind Laptop blinds PIN-derived messages before asking phones.
3. Phones Phones rate-limit and sign blinded requests without seeing the PIN.
4. Shares Laptop decrypts enough Shamir shares returned by phones.
5. Vault PIN + laptop device factor + threshold secret unwrap the vault key.
