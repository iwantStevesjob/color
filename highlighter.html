Drag across text

This demo blocks the native mobile text selection handles and replaces them with a custom highlighter interaction. Touch down on the text, drag your thumb across the words, and the page paints the characters underneath your finger.

The browser is no longer selecting text, so Android and iPhone should not show the little selection handles. The highlight you see here is just normal HTML styling applied by JavaScript as your finger moves.

Edge gestures are harder. A webpage can reduce accidental navigation gestures, but iPhone Safari can still reserve the far-left screen edge for browser back navigation. Start your drag inside the page, not from the bezel.
